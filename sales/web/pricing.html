Everything you need to know about waRpcoRE pricing

How does the free trial work? +

Your 14-day free trial includes full access to all Team plan features. No credit card required. After the trial, you can choose to upgrade or downgrade to any plan.

Can I change my plan at any time? +

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes are prorated automatically, and you'll only pay the difference for the remainder of your billing cycle.

What cloud providers do you support? +

waRpcoRE supports AWS, Google Cloud Platform, Microsoft Azure, and Kubernetes. We're constantly adding support for new cloud providers and services.

Do you offer volume discounts? +

Yes! Enterprise customers with large teams get custom pricing with significant volume discounts. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific needs.

Is my data secure? +

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption, SOC 2 compliant infrastructure, and follow strict security practices. Your cloud credentials are never stored on our servers.

Can I run waRpcoRE on-premises? +

Yes! Enterprise customers can deploy waRpcoRE on their own infrastructure for maximum security and compliance. This includes air-gapped environments.
